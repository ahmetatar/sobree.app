Sobree is designed to support a personal and sensitive journey. For that reason, the app is intended to collect and process only the information reasonably needed to provide its features, maintain reliability, and support user-requested functionality.

  • Tracker details, milestones, check-ins, reflections, journal entries, settings, and similar content entered in the app.

  • Optional wellness-related information made available through Apple Health, if permission is granted.

2. Information generated through app use

  • Purchase, subscription, and entitlement status made available through Apple StoreKit systems.

Health-related information is intended to support user-facing app features and is not described on this page as being used for advertising purposes.
