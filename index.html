Privacy Policy

We aim to collect only the information reasonably necessary to operate the app, provide features you request, maintain your purchases, and improve reliability and safety.

1. Information you may provide

  • App content you enter, such as tracker details, check-ins, reflections, journal entries, milestones, settings, and similar wellness-related records.
  • Optional information you choose to connect from Apple Health, if you grant HealthKit permission.
  • Support messages or emails you send directly to us.

2. Information generated through use of the app

  • Purchase and subscription status provided through Apple's StoreKit systems.
  • Basic technical or sync-related state needed for local storage, iCloud synchronization, widgets, notifications, or account recovery flows.
  • Diagnostic information made available through Apple platforms if needed to investigate crashes, sync failures, abuse, or security issues.

3. How information is used

  • To provide the app's core features.
  • To store your records on-device and, when applicable, sync them across your Apple devices.
  • To enable widgets, reminders, and personalized in-app experiences.
  • To verify purchases, restore premium access, and manage subscription-related access.
  • To maintain app security, fix bugs, and respond to support requests.

4. Health data

If sobree requests access to Apple Health, that access is optional. Health information is only accessed after you grant permission through Apple's authorization flow. If permission is denied or later revoked, related features may become limited or unavailable.

We do not use health information for advertising. Health information is used only to support app features you choose to enable.

5. iCloud and Apple services

sobree may rely on Apple-provided services such as CloudKit, iCloud, StoreKit, WidgetKit, UserNotifications, and HealthKit. When sync is enabled, app data may be transmitted to and stored within Apple's systems in connection with your Apple account and device environment.

6. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not intend to share your app content with advertising networks or unrelated third parties.

Information may be disclosed only when reasonably necessary to:

  • operate Apple platform services used by the app;
  • comply with law, regulation, or valid legal process;
  • detect, prevent, or address fraud, abuse, security, or technical issues;
  • protect the rights, safety, or property of users, the developer, or others.

7. Data retention

Data is generally retained for as long as needed to provide the app and related features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Retention may vary depending on whether data is stored only locally on your device, synchronized through your personal iCloud environment, or included in support communications.

8. Your choices

  • You can choose whether to grant permissions such as notifications or Apple Health access.
  • You can stop using sync-related features by disabling the relevant Apple account or app settings where available.
  • You can delete app content from within the app where deletion tools are provided, or by removing the app and related local data from your device.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of support correspondence or other data we directly control, subject to legal or operational limits.

9. Children

sobree is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to consent to data processing in their region unless explicitly stated otherwise on the App Store listing.

10. International users

If you use the app outside the developer's home country, your information may be processed in other countries depending on Apple infrastructure, hosting, support tools, or your own account configuration.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
